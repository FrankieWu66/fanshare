# The Assignment

Text each of your 10 friends **individually** (not a group chat) and say:

> "I'm building a product where you can trade tokens representing NBA players — price goes up when people buy, down when people sell, and there's a stat tracker showing what each player is actually worth based on their performance. Would you use this? What's the first thing you'd want to do?"

Record their responses here. Note the exact words they use.
